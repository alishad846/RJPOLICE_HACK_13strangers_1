Rajasthan Police Feedback Chatbot

Welcome to the Rajasthan Police Feedback Chatbot repository! This project is designed to facilitate efficient communication between citizens and the Rajasthan Police, allowing users to provide feedback, report incidents, and access information seamlessly through a chatbot interface.

Features

24/7 Accessibility: The chatbot is available round the clock, ensuring users can submit feedback or report incidents at any time.
User-Friendly Interface: The chatbot boasts an intuitive design for easy navigation, making it accessible to users of all backgrounds.
Anonymous Feedback: Users have the option to submit feedback anonymously, prioritizing user privacy and encouraging open communication.
Incident Reporting: The chatbot streamlines the process for reporting various incidents, including traffic violations and suspicious activities.
Service Improvement: Feedback collected through the chatbot contributes to ongoing efforts by the Rajasthan Police to enhance the quality of their services.
